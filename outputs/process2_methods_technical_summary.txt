4. FEATURE EXTRACTION
- Final Process-2 modeling features (13): word_count, sentence_count, type_token_ratio, filler_count, filler_ratio, mean_clause_length, content_density, noun_ratio, verb_ratio, adjective_ratio, adverb_ratio, pronoun_ratio, determiner_ratio.
- Formulas from code:
  - type_token_ratio = unique tokens / total tokens from cleaned transcript.
  - filler_count = count(tokens in {um, uh, er, well, like}).
  - filler_ratio = filler_count / total tokens.
  - mean_clause_length = sentence-average of token_count / max(1, finite predicate count).
  - content_density = content-word tokens / total non-space tokens, where content words are NOUN, VERB, ADJ, ADV.
  - noun_ratio, verb_ratio, adjective_ratio, adverb_ratio, pronoun_ratio, determiner_ratio = POS counts / total non-space tokens.
  - word_count = whitespace-token count of cleaned transcript.
  - sentence_count = included in the final Process-2 artifact feature set.
- Tokenizer: whitespace split on cleaned transcript.
- Sentence splitter/POS tagger: spaCy en_core_web_sm.
- Content-word categories: NOUN, VERB, ADJ, ADV.
- Filler list: um, uh, er, well, like.
- mean_clause_length calculation: spaCy sentences, count tokens and finite predicates, average token_count / max(1, predicate_count).
- semantic_coherence: computed in src/feature_extraction but excluded from the final Process-2 model feature set.
